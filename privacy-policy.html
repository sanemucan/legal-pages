🎮 Play Tots Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Summary for Parents: Play Tots does NOT collect any personal information from children or adults. Our app is completely safe, ad-free, and contains no tracking whatsoever.

1. Introduction

Play Tots ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through our mobile application Play Tots, designed for children ages 2-4 years old.

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and other applicable privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information.

Our app:

  • Does NOT require registration, login, or account creation
  • Does NOT collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any contact information
  • Does NOT collect photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Does NOT collect location data
  • Does NOT collect device identifiers or IP addresses
  • Does NOT use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Does NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • Does NOT contain any advertising

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Play Tots is designed specifically for children ages 2-4 years old. We are fully committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Since we do not collect any personal information from children or adults, no parental consent is required to use our app.

We do not:

  • Collect personal information from children under 13
  • Allow children to make personal information publicly available
  • Entice children with games or prizes to disclose personal information
  • Share any information with third parties

4. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland:

Since we do not collect, process, or store any personal data, the typical GDPR requirements regarding data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, etc.) do not apply. There is simply no personal data to access, correct, or delete.

5. Third-Party Services

Play Tots does NOT integrate with any third-party services that collect data. We do not use:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Crash reporting tools that collect personal data

6. In-App Purchases

Play Tots offers optional subscription purchases through the Apple App Store. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple, and we do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles payment data.

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no personal data to secure. All app settings and preferences (such as sound settings) are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us or any third party.

8. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data because we do not collect any personal data.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We encourage parents to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting children's privacy.

10. Parental Rights

Under COPPA, parents have certain rights regarding their children's personal information. However, since Play Tots does not collect any personal information, there is no data to review, delete, or modify.

If you have any concerns about your child's privacy while using our app, please contact us using the information below.
